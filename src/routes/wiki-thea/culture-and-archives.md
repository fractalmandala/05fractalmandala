Not every raw source belongs neatly to politics, theology, or ecology; some capture everyday life, artistic memory, sport, or the state of the archive itself. This page gathers those residual but important materials, which help round out the setting described elsewhere in [[thea-overview]], [[history-of-thea]], and [[species-and-peoples]].

## Source Summaries
- **dance and music**: Notes a visual or archival source focused on artistic depictions of dance and music across Thean history.
- **pilings**: Describes a three-versus-three orbital sport shared across all hominid species and celebrated through a galaxy cup.
- **kadorran technician**: Records a brief contemporary vignette centered on a technician working in Kadorra.
- **unsorted**: Marks a miscellaneous collection of unsorted images and depictions.
