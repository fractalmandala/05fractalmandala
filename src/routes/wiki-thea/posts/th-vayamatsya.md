**Kingdom:** Animalia
**Phylum:** Chordata
**Class:** Actinopterygii
**Order:** Vayamatsyiformes
**Family:** Vayamatsyidae
**Genus:** Vayamatsya

The Vayamatsya, a unique and mysterious piscine species documented during the Trithan Command, is one of the most intriguing inhabitants of Thea's vast and diverse galaxy. Known for their extraordinary habitat and biological characteristics, the Vayamatsya captivate the imagination of biologists, ecologists, and the general populace alike.

Vayamatsyas are characterized by their ethereal, jellyfish-like appearance. They possess translucent, bioluminescent bodies that emit a soft, otherworldly glow, illuminating their atmospheric surroundings. Their size can vary, with some individuals growing to remarkable dimensions, akin to the larger aquamarine jellyfish found on aquatic planets.

Uniquely, Vayamatsyas do not inhabit water bodies; instead, they are found floating in the atmospheres of certain planets in Thea. This extraordinary adaptation allows them to drift gracefully through the air, feeding on microscopic organisms and gases that constitute their diet.

Their movements are slow and graceful, often synchronized in what appears to be a delicate aerial dance. Vayamatsyas are most active during specific times of the day, aligning their movements with the rhythms of their planet's environmental cycles.
