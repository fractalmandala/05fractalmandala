The Fourth Great War, remembered as one of the most dreaded and catastrophic conflicts in Thean history, was a pivotal event that nearly led to the destruction of Kadorra and the extinction of the Gaurushas. This war, between hominids and the Prakas, is marked by its scale and the level of devastation it caused.

##### history

The war saw numerous large-scale battles, both in space and on planetary surfaces, showcasing advanced warfare technologies and tactics. The Prakas' use of advanced weapons technology on Gokarna led to the extinction of the Gaurushas, and other weaponry deployed nearly destroyed the planet of Kadorra.

It is deeply etched in the collective memory of Thean species as a symbol of survival against overwhelming odds, more so because of the Fifth Great War, where the hominids extracted their revenge. The war's dramatic and tragic nature has inspired various forms of art and literature, reflecting on themes of resilience, tragedy, and the horrors of war.
