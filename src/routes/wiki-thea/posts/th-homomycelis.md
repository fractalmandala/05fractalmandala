Homo Mycelis represents one of the six hominid species in the Thea galaxy, known for their distinctive genetic makeup that includes a significant proportion of mycelis DNA. This species carries a unique heritage, blending human and mycelis characteristics, which plays a significant role in their identity and culture. They have no name for themselves, and are known simply by their scientific name. 

Homo mycelis carry about 40% Mycelis DNA, a result of unknown events in their ancient history. This shows in their distinct physical appearance.

In their tradition, they believe themselves to be directly created by the Ritakartas, divine beings in Thean theology. Their interactions with other species, both hominid and non-hominid, are influenced by their unique heritage and cultural beliefs.
