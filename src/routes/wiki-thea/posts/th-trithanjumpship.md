The Trithan Jumpship is a creation borne from the genius of Yaktuki Tritha, one of the legendary Tritha twins. Designed to overcome the limitations of traditional space travel, these jumpships can instantaneously move between distant star systems, a feat achieved through the pioneering use of quantum entanglement propulsion technology.

Trithan Jumpships revolutionized interstellar travel in Thea, drastically reducing travel time between star systems. They were initially used for high-priority diplomatic missions and critical cargo transport, eventually becoming a symbol of Thean technological advancement.

The introduction of the Trithan Jumpship had a profound impact on Thean society, bringing distant worlds and cultures closer. It facilitated a new era of interstellar cooperation and exchange, significantly shaping the political and social landscape of the galaxy.

##### technical specification

Quantum entangled propulsion allows the jumpship to create and utilize quantum-entangled particles to achieve instantaneous travel between two points in space. A micro-singular reactor at the heart of the jumpship harnesses the energy of a contained micro-black hole, providing nearly limitless energy to power the ship's systems. As a defensive measure, the jumpship can generate a powerful electromagnetic pulse to disrupt incoming projectiles and electronic systems of potential threats.

##### history
Trithan Jumpships were instrumental in facilitating the historic Thean Satsang, where leaders from across Thea convened to discuss peace and cooperation. During the Vinaga Crisis, Trithan Jumpships were deployed for rapid-response rescue operations, saving thousands of lives across affected star systems. These ships have been used for groundbreaking scientific explorations, studying phenomena like black holes, supernovas, and uncharted galaxies.
