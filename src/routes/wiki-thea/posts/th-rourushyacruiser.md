The Rourushya Cruiser is a testament to the resilience of the Rourushya species. These vessels were initially designed as a part of a planetary evacuation plan following the near-destruction and then devastation of their homeworld during the Fourth Great War. The rapid development of these cruisers was spearheaded by the Rourushya Planetary Engineering Corps.

These cruisers played a pivotal role in the survival of the Rourushya species. They served as both a means of escape during the Fourth Great War and as mobile habitats for the Rourushya refugees in the aftermath of their planet's devastation. The flexibility and robustness of the design allowed the Rourushya people to establish temporary colonies in space while searching for a new planetary home.

The Rourushya Cruiser is not just a vessel; it symbolizes hope and endurance for the Rourushya people. The design incorporates cultural motifs reflecting the Rourushya spirit. The interiors are adorned with art and symbols from Rourushya mythology, serving as a reminder of their lost home and the resilience of their spirit.

##### technical specification

Core reactors are a marvel of Rourushya engineering, providing immense power to sustain the life support systems and propulsion for extended periods. Gravitational field generators, developed as a defensive mechanism against asteroid impacts and enemy fire, these generators create a protective field around the cruiser, deflecting incoming threats. The Rourushya Cruisers employ advanced AI systems for plotting courses, avoiding hazards, and managing complex flight dynamics.
