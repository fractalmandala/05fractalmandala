Bolla is a cherished fruit native to Vaunaki planet. Renowned for its popularity among the Vaunaki people, the bolla holds significant cultural and historical importance. It has been at the center of both sustenance and conflict. The fruit's value was so great that it led to ancient wars among Vaunaki ancestor tribes over access to bolla groves.

It is a staple in Vaunaki cuisine, used in various dishes and traditional recipes. It also plays a role in Vaunaki rituals and ceremonies, either as an offering or a symbol of prosperity. Efforts to cultivate and preserve Bolla groves are always a priority, considering its historical and cultural value, and it is rarely traded offworld.

Bolla, more than just a fruit, is a symbol of Vaunaki identity and history. Its role in past conflicts and its continued cultural importance make it a unique element of Vaunaki society. As a cherished aspect of their heritage, the bolla fruit embodies the traditions, tastes, and tales of the Vaunakis, enriching the diverse culinary and cultural landscape of Thea.
