Thea, a galaxy teeming with life, culture, and history, is a vast and diverse cosmic entity home to the six hominid species living under the sanatana sarvatra system, and the reptilian sapient species of Vinagas. Besides these, Thea is encountered by mysterious alien species like Dirghams, Prakas and Antas. This galaxy is not just a cluster of stars and planets; it's a complex froth of civilizations, each contributing to the rich and intricate narrative of Thean existence.

##### description
Thea comprises numerous star systems, each harboring a variety of planets, moons, and other celestial bodies. The galaxy's geography is as diverse as its inhabitants, featuring planets with varied climates and ecosystems – from the lush, verdant worlds of the Kapusha to the technologically advanced urban landscapes of the Avinaki.

Some planets in Thea are known for their unique natural phenomena, such as the luminous auroras of Kadorra and the crystalline oceans of Ormaunk. Thea's celestial canvas is a spectacle of cosmic beauty, with nebulae, supernovas, and black holes adding to the grandeur. Major inhabited planets are:

- Kadorra, home to the Manushya species.
- Prithviyah, home to the Avinaki species.
- Arkindha, home to the Kapusha species.
- Ormaunk, home to the Vaunaki species.
- Ditana, quantafactory.

##### inhabitants
Thea is a melting pot of civilizations, each with its own distinct culture, history, and social structure. The six hominid species – the Kadorrans, Vaunakis, Avinakis, Kapushas, Rourushyas, and Homo Mycelis – form the core of Thean society, each contributing unique perspectives and achievements. The other prominence is of the reptilian species - Vinaga.

The galaxy is rich in cultural heritage, with a plethora of languages, traditions, and art forms. Thean literature, music, and dance are particularly notable, reflecting the deep spiritual and philosophical underpinnings of Thean life.

##### culture and media
The hominid species of Thea are united under the sanatana sarvatra system. They live in mostly peaceful and mutually exclusive co-existence with the Vinagas, who also follow aspects that are common in sanatana sarvatra system.

The galaxy has seen empires rise and fall, with periods of unified governance under figures like the Deo Samrats, and times of fragmented rule.
