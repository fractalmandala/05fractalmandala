**Kingdom:** Plantae  
**Phylum:** Angiosperms  
**Class:** Dicotyledonae  
**Order:** Mangiferales  
**Family:** Mangiferae  
**Genus:** Fructus

Jackedmango, a fruit from the planet Kadorra, is renowned for its exceptional taste, holding a special place in both Kadorran culture and the broader Thean culinary landscape. Its popularity transcends planetary boundaries, making it a symbol of Kadorran cultural centrality.

The jackedmango is lauded for its delicious taste, possessing a highly desirable flavor profile. Its cultivation is an important aspect of Kadorran agriculture, given its popularity and economic significance. In the pre-technological age, jackedmangos accounted for a significant portion of Kadorran exports, underlining their economic importance.

The fruit's popularity extends beyond Kadorra, with many species across Thea appreciating its taste and quality. Even the Vaunaki, known for their pride in their own produce, acknowledge the jackedmango's superior taste.
