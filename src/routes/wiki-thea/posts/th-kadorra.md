Kadorra is a term that uniquely refers to both a planet and its largest region/megapolis, also known as Kadorra Prime. The native hominids of Kadorra, Manushyas, are also referred to by other hominids in the galaxy as Kadorrans. It is a hub of significant political, cultural and historical importance, playing a pivotal role in the broader narrative of Thean civilization. Add to this the fact that it is the birthplace of the Tritha twins, and Kadorra is effectively the general culture capital of the galaxy.

As a planet, Kadorra hosts a variety of ecosystems, contributing to its rich biodiversity. It has been at the forefront of several Great Wars, shaping its history and legacy in Thean civilization. Kadorra's economy is robust, bolstered by its agricultural produce, such as the famed Jackedmango, and technological advancements.
