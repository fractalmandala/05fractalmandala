Pilings is a unique and widely celebrated orbital sport, played between two teams of three players each. Renowned for being the only sport enjoyed by all hominid species in the galaxy, it holds the distinguished status of having a galaxy-wide tournament, the Thean Shastra, held biennially.

##### description

Pilings is played in a specially designed orbital arena, a spherical structure floating in space. The arena is equipped with various obstacles, platforms, and goals, strategically placed to enhance the game's complexity and excitement.

The primary objective in Pilings is to score points by launching a metallic ball into the opponent's goal. The goals are located on opposite ends of the arena and are protected by various automated defense systems.

Players are equipped with jetpacks and magnetic gloves, allowing them to navigate the zero-gravity environment and manipulate the ball. The game demands exceptional agility, strategic thinking, and teamwork, as players maneuver through the arena, passing the ball and outmaneuvering opponents. Players wear specialized suits designed for mobility and safety in zero gravity. The suits are equipped with jetpacks for movement and magnetic gloves for handling the ball. Helmets with HUD (Heads-Up Display) provide real-time game information and communication capabilities.

The ball used in Pilings is a dense, metallic sphere, responsive to magnetic manipulation. Its design ensures visibility and durability under the strenuous conditions of the game.

Pilings teams are diverse, often comprising players from different hominid species, showcasing the sport's role in promoting inter-species camaraderie and understanding. Players are celebrated for their skill and sportsmanship, with many becoming prominent figures in their respective societies.

##### culture and media

Pilings is more than a sport; it's a cultural phenomenon that unites the galaxy. It symbolizes the shared passion and competitive spirit across different worlds and species, fostering a sense of community and solidarity. The Thean Shastra, the most prestigious tournament in Pilings, is a major galactic event, attracting spectators and media from across the galaxy. It's not just a sporting event but a celebration of galactic diversity and unity.

The sport has driven technological advancements, particularly in zero-gravity navigation and safety equipment. The design and construction of Pilings arenas have also contributed to the development of orbital structures and habitats.
