The Dirgham species holds a unique and formidable position in Thean history. The first non-hominid sapient species encountered by the Manushyas, their emergence and subsequent actions significantly shaped the historical narrative of Thea, particularly through their involvement in the Great Wars.

##### first encounter

The Dirgham came to prominence following their attention towards the Kadorrans' use of nuclear technology during the First Great War. Their interactions with the Kadorrans and other species marked a pivotal moment in Thean interstellar relations and conflict.

They were central antagonists in the Second and Third Great Wars, posing significant threats to the hominid species. Their advanced technology and aggressive tactics made them formidable opponents in these conflicts.

##### description

Specific descriptions of their physical characteristics are significantly different from hominid species. The Dirgham's advanced technological capabilities, especially in warfare, were a key factor in their historical roles. Their role in history has made them a symbol of external existential threats in Thean cultural narratives.
