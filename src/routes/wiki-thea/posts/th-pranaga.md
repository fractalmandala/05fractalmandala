The Pranaga stand as one of the most awe-inspiring and enigmatic entities within the galaxy of Thea. Originating from the traditions of the Vinaga and Naga homeworlds, the Pranagas are deeply entrenched in the cosmic lore of these species. They are revered as the cosmic progenitors, creators of life, and pivotal figures in the spiritual and cultural fabric of their followers.

##### description
The Pranagas do not have a society in the conventional sense, as they are considered higher-dimensional beings existing beyond the regular constraints of space and time. Their influence, however, permeates through the cultures of the Vinaga and Naga species. Temples dedicated to the Pranagas are common, with intricate murals depicting their cosmic feats and their role in the creation of life.

The Pranagas are seen as the ultimate arbiters of cosmic balance, transcending the material world and influencing the course of events in subtle, often mystical ways. Festivals, rituals, and ceremonies in their honor are central to Vinaga and Naga cultures, often involving elaborate dances, offerings, and recitations of ancient texts describing their deeds and teachings.

##### religious significance
According to the Vinaga scrolls, the Pranagas were the first beings to emerge from the primordial chaos, bringing order and life to the universe. They are credited with the creation of the first stars, planets, and the foundational elements of life.

In Naga traditions, the Pranagas are considered as the guardians of cosmic knowledge, bestowing wisdom upon those who seek it with pure intent. They are also associated with the cycle of creation and destruction, embodying the eternal flow of the universe's life force.

##### depiction
Pranagas, as described in the ancient Vinaga scrolls, are colossal serpentine beings with shimmering scales that reflect the cosmos' hues. Their eyes are said to hold the depth of nebulas, glowing with an ethereal light. Each Pranaga has a unique pattern of scales, which is believed to represent different aspects of the universe - from star systems to entire galaxies. Their presence is often accompanied by an aura of otherworldly energy, and they are capable of manifesting in various sizes, from the enormous, spanning galaxies, to a more approachable, humanoid size for interactions with other beings.

##### culture and media
The Pranagas' influence extends beyond mere mythology. Their teachings and the principles they embody form the basis of many philosophical and ethical systems within the Vinaga and Naga cultures. They represent the pursuit of knowledge, the understanding of the cosmos, and the quest for spiritual enlightenment.

In recent times, interest in the Pranagas has surged among other species in Thea, with scholars and theologians seeking to understand their nature and the truths they represent. This has led to a cultural exchange, where ideas and beliefs about the Pranagas are shared and discussed among various species, enriching the spiritual tapestry of Thea.
