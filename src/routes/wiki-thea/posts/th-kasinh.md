**Order:** Feliformia  
**Family:** Kasinthidae

Ka'sinh represents a category of apex predators found on planets other than Kadorra, particularly dominant on the homeworlds of the Kapusha and Rourushya. Their presence as top predators significantly influences the ecological dynamics of these planets.

##### description and habitat

As apex predators, Ka'sinh are characterized by their strength, agility, and effective hunting strategies. Ka'sinh play a crucial role in maintaining the ecological balance on their respective planets, controlling the population of other species.

##### culture and media

In the cultures of Kapusha and Rourushya, Ka'sinh are seen as symbols of natural power and the untamed aspects of their worlds. Their formidable nature makes them subjects of local mythology and folklore, inspiring tales of bravery and respect for nature.
