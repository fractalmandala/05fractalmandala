**Kingdom:** Fungi
**Phylum:** Basidiomycota
**Class:** Agaricomycetes
**Order:** Myceliales
**Family:** Trithamycetaceae
**Genus:** Trithamycelium

Mycelis trithas, named in honor of the legendary Tritha twins, is a unique and culturally significant species within the Mycelis genus. Native to the region of Kadorra on the planet Kadorra, this species is renowned for its entheogenic properties and its deep connection to the local cultural and spiritual practices.

It is distinguished by its elegant, ethereal appearance. The caps are usually a vibrant blue or purple, with a translucent quality that seems to shimmer in certain lights. Thriving in the shadowed and mystical landscapes of Kadorra, mycelis trithas prefers humid and dimly lit environments. Its growth is particularly abundant in areas imbued with historical and spiritual significance.

##### culture and media

Mycelis trithas is known for its strong entheogenic properties, inducing altered states of consciousness when consumed. These effects are deeply integrated into various ritualistic and meditative practices in Kadorran culture. Named after the Tritha twins, this species holds a special place in Kadorran lore. It symbolizes the twins' deep connection to the mystical and spiritual realms.
