Vaunakastha, the resplendent capital city of the Vaunaki homeworld of Ormaunk, stands as a beacon of cultural richness, architectural ingenuity, and historical depth in Thea. As a city that has witnessed numerous epochal events and served as a cradle for the Vaunaki civilization, Vaunakastha is a symbol of resilience, pride, and the enduring spirit of its inhabitants.

Nestled amidst rugged mountains and lush valleys, Vaunakastha is a marvel of urban planning and environmental harmony. The city's architecture is a blend of traditional Vaunaki designs and modern engineering, featuring towering structures, expansive public squares, and intricate water systems that reflect its inhabitants' reverence for nature.

Vaunakastha is the heart of Vaunaki culture, home to numerous festivals, arts, and traditions. The city's theaters, galleries, and museums showcase the best of Vaunaki art, from ancient artifacts to contemporary works. Traditional dance and music are integral to the city's cultural life, with performances and celebrations held regularly in public spaces.

##### history
The city is steeped in history, having been the center of several major events in Thean history. It is renowned as the site of multiple rebirths of maharishi Triarka and as the final home of the Tritha twins, Yaktuki and Vaktuki, making it a place of great spiritual and historical pilgrimage.

##### culture and media
As the capital, Vaunakastha is the political heart of the Vaunaki world. It hosts the main governmental institutions and diplomatic missions from other planets. Economically, the city thrives on trade, technology, and tourism, attracting visitors and business from across Thea.

Vaunakastha's architecture is a testament to the Vaunaki's advanced engineering skills and aesthetic sensibilities. The buildings are constructed to withstand the region's environmental challenges while also reflecting the Vaunaki's connection with their planet. The city is renowned for its sustainable practices, including renewable energy sources and waste management systems.

The city houses several ancient temples, including the famed Temple of Othrank, which is a central location for spiritual discourse and worship. These temples are not only places of religious significance but also serve as centers for community gatherings and philosophical discussions.
