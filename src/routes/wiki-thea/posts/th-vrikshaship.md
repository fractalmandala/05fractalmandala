The Vrikshaship, a unique creation in the galaxy of Thea, was designed by maharishi Triarka at the behest of Samrat Arhadeo. Its purpose was to traverse the galaxy, collecting and preserving diverse lifeforms and ecosystems threatened by environmental changes or galactic conflicts.

Vrikshaships serve as mobile conservation units, preserving the genetic and ecological diversity of Thea. They are equipped with state-of-the-art facilities for studying and nurturing various species, playing a vital role in ecological research and preservation efforts. The Vrikshaship is not just a vessel but a symbol of the Thean commitment to environmental stewardship and the preservation of life. Its presence in any star system is a sign of hope and a commitment to the protection of the natural world.
