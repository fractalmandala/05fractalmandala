The Avinaki are one of the six hominid species inhabiting the Thean galaxy, known for their technological prowess and unique societal structure. They are distinguished by their advanced civilization and have a significant impact on the galactic dynamics of Thea. They are known for their use of advanced technology, which is integrated into their physical being and used extensively in their daily lives.

Avinaki society is organized along a rigid, centrally-administered class system, with a highly structured and stratified social order. Unique among the hominid species, Avinaki children are not raised by their biological parents. Like with most things, they possess an institutional approach to child-rearing and consider upbringing by biological parents to be most primitive. Their unique approaches to social structure, child-rearing, and their remarkable technological achievements not only define their own civilization but also impact the broader dynamics of Thea. 

##### technology

A testament to their technological advancement, the Avinaki cruiser is capable of transporting 10 million passengers across the galaxy, highlighting their capabilities in space travel and engineering. The Avinaki Harvesters - star-sized machines demonstrate their ability to harvest energy from celestial bodies, further cementing their status as a technologically advanced civilization.

The Avinaki's technological superiority positions them as a key player in the political and economic landscape of the Thea galaxy. Their interactions with other hominid species and their involvement in galactic affairs are significant, given their advanced status.
