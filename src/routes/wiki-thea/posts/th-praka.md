The Praka, a species enveloped in mystery and intrigue, are one of the most enigmatic and menacing presences within the galaxy of Thea. Known for their pivotal role as antagonists in the Fourth and Fifth Great Wars, the Praka have become synonymous with fear and destruction among the hominid species of Thea.

##### description
Prakas are characterized by their chitinous exoskeletons and segmented bodies, which bear a resemblance to the arthropods of ancient Earth. They possess multiple limbs, each ending in various appendages suitable for manipulation or combat. Their skin tones range from dark obsidian to a reflective silver, allowing for camouflage in various environments. The most distinctive feature of the Praka is their bioluminescent markings, which not only serve as a means of communication but also intimidate opponents by displaying intricate patterns during conflict.

Praka society is built around a rigid hive-like structure, with each individual assigned specific roles that contribute to the overall wellbeing of their community. Unlike typical insectoid societies, the Praka exhibit a high level of individual intelligence, combined with a collective consciousness that guides their actions and decisions.

The Praka possess largely advanced technology, compared to the Thean hominids, particularly in terraforming and space travel. The Praka Terraformer, a planet-converting behemoth, is infamous for its capability to render entire planets uninhabitable to hominid species. This technology played a devastating role in the Fourth Great War, leading to the extinction of the Rourushya species and the uninhabitability of their homeworld, .

Their space-faring technology is equally impressive, with Praka Cruisers being equipped with advanced cloaking and armament capabilities. These ships were pivotal in their invasions and were feared across Thea for their stealth and destructive power.

**Role in Thean History**
The Praka first appeared in Thean history during the Fourth Great War, known as the most dreaded and horrific conflict remembered by the hominid species. They launched a surprise attack on Kadorra, leading to massive destruction and near extinction of several hominid species. Their motives remained unclear, but their actions left a lasting impact on Thean history, shaping the political and military strategies of various species for centuries to come.

In the Fifth Great War, also known as Ritamdeo's Revenge, the Praka faced a significant defeat. This war marked the turning point in Thean history, as the united front of hominid species under the leadership of Samrat Ritamdeo finally pushed back the Praka, leading to the near extinction of the Praka themselves and the destruction of their homeworld.

**Current Status**
Following their defeat in the Fifth Great War, the Praka have not been encountered in Thean space. Their current status is a subject of much speculation, with some believing them to be rebuilding their forces in secret, while others consider them a fallen empire, unlikely to regain their former glory. Their legacy, however, continues to influence the geopolitical landscape of Thea, serving as a reminder of the destructive potential of unchecked ambition and the importance of unity in the face of existential threats.
