**Order:** Carnivoramorph

Gajakra, apex predator of the planet Ormaunk, holds a notorious reputation for its role in the ecosystem. This species is particularly recognized for causing the extinction of the abudha-gajas, showcasing its formidable predatory capabilities.

##### description and habitat

Gajakras have a thoroughly unassuming appearance, not looking like a predator species at all. But as apex predators, they possess formidable strength and adaptability. They are characterized in behavior by sudden, unpredictable sprints of aggression and predation.

##### culture and media

In the lore and culture of the Vaunakis of Ormaunk, gajakras are viewed as symbols of raw power, danger, and the unforgiving nature of the natural world. They influence local legends, cautionary tales and culture practices related to wildlife and survival.

The gajakras stand as a powerful testament to the dynamic and often perilous nature of planetary ecosystems in the Thea galaxy. As apex predators of Ormaunk, their influence extends beyond the ecological realm, impacting cultural narratives and scientific understanding. The legacy of their predatory behavior, particularly regarding the abudha-gajas, highlights the intricate and sometimes harsh realities of natural selection and species interaction in the cosmos.
