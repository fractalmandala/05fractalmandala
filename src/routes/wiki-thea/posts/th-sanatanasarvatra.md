The Sanatana Sarvatra System is a fundamental and universal doctrine that forms the bedrock of shared beliefs and practices among the six hominid species of Thea. The name literally means '*eternal omnipresent system.*'  This system is a comprehensive framework that unites various species under a common spiritual and ethical umbrella, promoting harmony, understanding, and mutual respect across diverse cultures and worlds.

##### principles
At its core, the sanatana sarvatra system is anchored in the concept of an eternal, universal order that governs all aspects of life and existence. It emphasizes the interconnectedness of all beings and the importance of maintaining balance and harmony within this cosmic order.

The system is built upon a series of principles that guide the moral, ethical, and spiritual conduct of its adherents. These principles include the pursuit of knowledge, the practice of compassion and empathy, and the importance of living in harmony with the natural world. It also underscores the significance of personal and collective responsibility in sustaining the cosmic order.

##### practices
The system manifests in a rich diversity of cultural and spiritual practices that are observed by the hominid species of Thea. These practices range from daily rituals and prayers to elaborate ceremonies and festivals that mark important cosmic events.

One of the central aspects of the system is the emphasis on meditation and contemplation as means of achieving spiritual enlightenment and understanding the deeper truths of the universe. Art, music, and dance are also integral to the system, serving as expressions of the divine and as tools for spiritual growth.

##### culture and media
Beyond its spiritual implications, the system has a profound impact on the governance and societal structures of the hominid species. It advocates for a form of governance that is just, compassionate, and in tune with the cosmic order. Leaders are expected to rule with wisdom and integrity, ensuring the well-being of all citizens and the environment.

The system also promotes social equality and justice, advocating for the eradication of social and economic disparities. It encourages the development of societies where all beings can thrive and contribute to the collective well-being.

The Sanatana Sarvatra System plays a pivotal role in shaping interplanetary relations within Thea. It serves as a common ground for different species, facilitating diplomatic engagements and cooperative endeavors. The shared beliefs and values of the system foster a sense of unity and mutual respect among the diverse inhabitants of Thea.
