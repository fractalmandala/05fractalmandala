The vibudha-gaja, a once formidable and now extinct carnivorous gaja species from the Vaunaki homeworld of Ormaunk, represents a fascinating chapter in the galaxy's natural history. Known for their unique dietary habits and formidable presence, the vibudha-gajas were a species that left a significant mark on the ecosystem and culture of their native planet.

##### description
Vibudha-gajas were large, imposing creatures, distinct for their carnivorous adaptation - a rare trait among gaja species. They possessed robust bodies with muscular limbs, enabling them to be formidable predators. Their skin was thick and tough, providing protection against the attacks of other predators and prey.

A key feature of the vibudha-gaja was its large, powerful jaws and sharp teeth, evolved specifically for a carnivorous diet. Unlike their herbivorous counterparts, vibudha-gajas had a more streamlined body, aiding in their agility and speed necessary for hunting.

##### behavior
Vibudha-gajas were apex predators in their environment, preying on a variety of species. Their hunting tactics were based on power and speed, often ambushing their prey in a display of strength and agility. Living in smaller herds, they exhibited complex social behaviors, including territory marking and collective hunting strategies. Their presence in the ecosystem played a crucial role in controlling the population of other species, maintaining a delicate ecological balance.
