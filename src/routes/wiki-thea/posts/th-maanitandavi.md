Ma'anitandavi is a deity found in the religious traditions of the Vaunaki and Kapusha species. This entity holds a distinct place in their spiritual beliefs, illustrating the rich diversity of Thean theology and the variations in divine interpretation across different cultures. In the traditions of the Vaunaki and Kapusha, Ma'anitandavi is viewed as a separate entity from other deities, particularly Kim'asta. But the Trithan Command suggests that Ma'anitandavi might be an aspect or form of Kim'asta, indicating a complex and layered understanding of this deity.
