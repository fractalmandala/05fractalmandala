Travel technology in Thea ranges from ordinary public infrastructure to legendary warcraft and biological conservation vessels. These sources together show how movement across planets and star systems underwrites trade, pilgrimage, war, settlement, and interspecies contact, making this page a practical companion to [[technology-and-industry]] and [[history-of-thea]].

## Source Summaries
- **avinaki cruiser**: Describes the Avinaki cruiser as a colossal passenger vessel able to move millions across the galaxy.
- **hopper**: Presents the hopper as a standard bulk cargo and passenger craft used widely across hominid planets.
- **intraplanet metro**: Summarizes metro rail as the most common mass-commute system on hominid worlds.
- **kadorran grandvimana**: Records the largest Kadorran fleet vessel and its decisive role in the final great war.
- **kadorran scrambler**: Describes a fast single-seat Kadorran fighter for high-speed interplanetary travel.
- **rourushya cruiser**: Notes the standard Rourushya cruiser and its importance in evacuation during the fourth great war.
- **trithan jumpship**: Presents Yaktuki Tritha's jumpship as a craft capable of instantaneous travel between star systems.
- **vaunaki starship**: Summarizes the Vaunaki starship as a large interstellar vessel central to exploration and colonization.
- **vinaga cruiser**: Describes the feared Vinaga cruiser as a warship of conquest, slave transport, and overwhelming military prestige.
- **vrikshaship**: Records Triarka's life-preserving vessel built for Arhadeo's conservation mission.
