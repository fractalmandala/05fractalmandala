The historical corpus centers on three recurring structures: the unifying role of the Deo dynasty, the catastrophic sequence of Great Wars, and the theological-political rupture introduced by the Tritha twins. These sources make history in Thea inseparable from religion, species conflict, and changing technologies, linking closely to [[rulers-and-sages]], [[species-and-peoples]], and [[technology-and-industry]].

## Dynastic and Epochal History
- **deo dynasty**: Summarizes the Grand Deo dynasty as the great unifying imperial house of Thea and patron of the shared hominid order through the first five great wars.
- **three great dialogues**: Records the deep-antiquity dialogues through which hominid peoples recognized the common core later called the sanatana sarvatra system.
- **trithan command**: Defines the Trithan Command both as the doctrinal program of the twins and as the entire era stretching from their rise to their victory in the final war.

## The Great Wars
- **first great war**: Describes an intra-Kadorran war remembered for the destruction of Ahurun and the resulting millennia of darkness.
- **second great war**: Covers the first Dirgham invasion and the rise of Sarvadeo as defender of the hominid worlds.
- **third great war**: Recounts the second Dirgham invasion, preserved especially in epic tradition and associated with heroes like Oldenki.
- **fourth great war**: Presents the first great conflict between Ritamdeo and the Prakas, including the near-destruction of Kadorra.
- **fifth great war**: Describes Ritamdeo's revenge and the near-annihilation of the Prakas, which became the last great military triumph of the dynasty.
- **final great war**: Frames the Trithan rebellion against Suradeo as the sixth and final great war and the event that ended the Deo dynasty.
