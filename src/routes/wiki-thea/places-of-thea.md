The raw place entries map Thea from galaxy scale down to pilgrimage cities, agricultural regions, and war-scarred industrial zones. These locations often matter less as neutral geography than as anchors for memory, theology, and political change, especially where they intersect with [[history-of-thea]], [[rulers-and-sages]], and [[species-and-peoples]].

## Source Summaries
- **kadorra**: Defines Kadorra as both a planet and its dominant megapolis, making it one of the principal geographic and political centers of Thea.
- **ormaunk**: Identifies Ormaunk as the Vaunaki homeworld and a key setting for Vaunaki ecology, religion, and urban life.
- **ahurun**: Describes Ahurun as a once-rural Kadorran region transformed into an arms center, then devastated by nuclear attack and remembered as a warning about militarization.
- **askini**: Presents Askini as Kadorra's breadbasket and a region also shaped by the rehabilitation and settlement of displaced Rourushya.
- **vasatas**: Marks Vasatas as a Kadorran megapolis and major pilgrimage site because of its link to the Tritha twins during their flight from Suradeo.
- **vaunakastha**: Describes Vaunakastha as Ormaunk's capital, a city of repeated Triarkan rebirth associations, and the site of the singular temple of Othrank.
