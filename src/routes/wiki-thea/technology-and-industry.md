The technological sources emphasize scale: planet-sized industry, star-sized harvesters, galactic conservation systems, and portals or terraformers that alter the conditions of life itself. Technology in Thea is not merely utilitarian; it reorganizes empire, migration, ecology, and war, connecting closely to [[history-of-thea]], [[transport-and-infrastructure]], and [[species-and-peoples]].

## Industrial and Scientific Systems
- **arhadeo conservatory**: Describes a galaxy-spanning preservation project dedicated to cataloguing and protecting lifeforms across Thea.
- **avinaki harvester**: Summarizes a star-scale Avinaki energy-harvesting structure that exemplifies their command of extreme engineering.
- **ditana shipbuilding dockyard**: Presents Ditana as the galaxy's great shipbuilding marvel, associated especially with Sarvadeo's era.
- **quantafactory**: Defines the quantafactory as a whole planet converted into productive industrial machinery, with Ditana as the iconic example.

## Terraforming, Portals, and War Machines
- **vaunaki portal**: Describes instantaneous teleportation technology originating in Vaunaki tradition but later possessed by all hominids.
- **vaunaki terraformer**: Summarizes the Vaunaki ability to convert uninhabitable worlds into habitable ones, supporting their pattern of expansion.
- **praka terraformer**: Presents the dreaded Praka terraformer as a weaponized planetary conversion system that made the Rourushya homeworld unlivable.
- **kadorran quarryship**: Records a standard Kadorran mining and cargo ship whose design became influential beyond Kadorra.

## Recovered Evidence
- **biofauna**: Notes an Earth-recovered repository of biological samples and evidence from Thea.
- **sapient life**: Records Earth-recovered evidence concerning sapient lifeforms and possible common ancestry.
- **technology**: Notes recovered technological blueprints and engineering drawings tied to the Thea corpus.
