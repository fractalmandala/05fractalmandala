chakravartin of legend and lore, who waged war on and defeated each of the pancha-gana tribes. possibly remembered as trasadasyu in the legends active among bharata bards. at its peak, his empire extended from east of ayodhya to the remote city of gandhara in the far west. it did not survive his death, which was under mysterious circumstances.
