the northern of the two cities founded by dushyanta aila, along the banks of the ganga. one of bharatavarsha's most ancient cities. a site of pilgrimage for people of the pancha-gana and the suryavansha alike. for the latter, it has special status as the site where bhagiratha resided for ten years.
