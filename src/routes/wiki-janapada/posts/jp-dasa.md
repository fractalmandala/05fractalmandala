the name given by bharatas to a conglomerate of enemy and mleccha tribes. dasa bands are composed of druhyus, danavas, anavas, shighras, shakas, hunas, yavanas and more. they have become rare in bharatavarsha since the defeat of their leader shambara at the hands of rajan divodasa.
