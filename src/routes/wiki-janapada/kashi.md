Large urban center and possibly bharatavarsha's most ancient city. It was abandoned in the previous eras and rediscovered by sunahotra, the younger son of chakravartin [[bharata]] during the latter's ashvamedha. Gifted by [[bharata]] to his daughter, who he named kashi in the city's honour.
