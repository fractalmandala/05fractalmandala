This page gathers the sources concerned with artificial intelligence, technological acceleration, platforms, blockchain, disruption, and future-facing civilizational systems. The notes range from practical observation to speculation, but the throughline is clear: technology matters most when viewed not as gadget novelty but as an expression of worldview, power, creativity, and the future of human civilization. Related topics: [[sveltekit-and-web-development]], [[design-art-and-digital-garden]], [[consciousness-reality-and-metaphysics]].

## Core Themes

- AI as transformative but still philosophically incomplete.
- Technology as civilizational choice rather than neutral tool.
- Future systems such as blockchain, bots, and interfaces to the sacred.
- Critical resistance to “disruption” myths and Big Tech ideology.
