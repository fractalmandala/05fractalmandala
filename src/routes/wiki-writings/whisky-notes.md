This page gathers the whisky material in the corpus, including both general reflections and bottle-specific tasting notes. Unlike the more civilizational or philosophical pages, this cluster is practical and sensory: it records preferences, flavor profiles, distillery context, and a personal journey through single malts, especially Scotch expressions. Related topics: [[poetry-aphorisms-and-personal-reflections]].

## Core Themes

- Personal whisky tasting archive.
- Preference formation through repeated sampling.
- Distillery context and style comparisons.
- Light educational notes for beginners.

## Source Summaries

- `indiansinglemalt.md` - Brief note on Indian single malt as a category or interest area.
- `whiskeyorwhisky.md` - Explains the historical and regional reasons for the spelling split between “whisky” and “whiskey.”

- `whiskeyreviewbalvenie.md` - Reviews Balvenie Doublewood with attention to sherry-cask finishing and Speyside character.
- `whiskeyreviewbenriachpeated21.md` - Reviews BenRiach Peated 21 as a rare, older peated Speyside experience.
- `whiskeyreviewglenfiddich12.md` - Reviews Glenfiddich 12 as a classic spice-and-fruit Speyside benchmark.
- `whiskeyreviewglenlivet12.md` - Reviews Glenlivet 12 as a textured, nutty, archetypal Speyside malt.
- `whiskeyreviewglenlivet15yo.md` - Reviews Glenlivet 15 French Oak Reserve from the perspective of a drinker usually cooler on Glenlivet.
- `whiskeyreviewglenlivetnadurra.md` - Highly favorable review of Glenlivet Nadurra as a natural, rounded Speyside malt.
- `whiskeyreviewlaphroaig10.md` - Reviews Laphroaig 10 through the lens of prior familiarity with peat and smoke.
- `whiskeyreviewlaphroaig18.md` - Reviews Laphroaig 18 as a more serious and expensive stage in the malt journey.
- `whiskeyreviewlaphroaigpx.md` - Reviews the PX Cask as a privileged and memorable tasting experience.
- `whiskeyreviewlaphroaigquarter.md` - Reviews Quarter Cask as the author’s true initiation into single malts.
- `whiskeyreviewlaphroaigselect.md` - Reviews Laphroaig Select with affection shaped by an established love of the distillery.
- `whiskeyreviewportcharlotte.md` - Reviews Port Charlotte PC11 while also dwelling on the distillery’s project and history.
- `whiskeyreviewsmokehead.md` - Reviews Smokehead as a deliberate, cultish smoky malt with Laphroaig-adjacent traits.
- `whiskeyreviewtomintoul.md` - Reviews Tomintoul 10 while also introducing the distillery and pronunciation.
- `whiskeyreviewwilliampeel12.md` - Reviews William Peel 12 despite the difficulty of finding reliable distillery information.
