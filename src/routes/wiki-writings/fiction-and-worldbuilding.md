This page gathers the speculative fiction, mythic retellings, sci-fi, dynastic stories, and internal plot notes found across the source set. The common thread is worldbuilding through civilizational texture: even the far-future stories often retain Dharmic, Sanskritic, or ancient Indian motifs, while the historical fiction treats remembered lineages and cosmologies as living narrative matter. Related topics: [[ancient-indian-history-and-chronology]], [[dharma-and-civilizational-consciousness]], [[psychedelics-and-sacred-experience]].

## Core Themes

- Historical fiction rooted in ancient Indian dynasties and Vedic memory.
- Science fiction infused with dharma, cosmic consciousness, and civilizational motifs.
- Internal plot scaffolds that show how the larger narratives are being built.
- Mythic and sacred imagination extended into genre fiction.
