This page gathers the writing on psychedelics as transformative experience, philosophical challenge, safety problem, and possible bridge to Vedic or Hindu ways of knowing. Across these sources, psychedelia is never treated as mere recreation; it is framed as a serious encounter with consciousness, mythic structure, risk, and altered ontologies. Related topics: [[consciousness-reality-and-metaphysics]], [[dharma-and-civilizational-consciousness]], [[fiction-and-worldbuilding]].

## Core Themes

- Psychedelics as initiatory and ontological experience.
- Harm reduction, discipline, and the ethics of use.
- McKenna as a guide, provocation, and comparative figure.
- Convergences between psychedelic visions and Hindu cosmology.

## Source Summaries
- [Bhartrhari and McKenna](/wiki-writings/posts/bhartrhariandmckenna) - Imagines a dialogue between Bhartrhari and Terence McKenna to explore language, psychedelia, and meaning.
- [Cardinal Rules](/wiki-writings/posts/cardinalrules) - Sets out practical and moral rules for engaging psychedelics responsibly, with unusual emphasis on consequences and hypocrisy.
- [Levels of Psychedelic Experience](/wiki-writings/posts/levelsofpsychedelicexperience) - Maps psychedelic states as ascending levels of intensity and significance.
- [McKenna](/wiki-writings/posts/mckenna) - Short contemplation on Terence McKenna’s courage, nature mysticism, and philosophical energy.
- [On Psychedelic and Hindu Consciousness](/wiki-writings/posts/on-psychedelic-and-hindu-consciousness) - Argues for strong parallels between psychedelic entities and Hindu cosmic categories.
- [Psychedelic Case](/wiki-writings/posts/psychedeliccase) - Suggests that Vedic rishis may have been seers in a sense compatible with psychedelic experience.
- [Psychedelic Experience](/wiki-writings/posts/psychedelicexperience) - Broader essay on psychedelics in culture, ritual, cognition, and sacred history.
- [Psychedelics](/wiki-writings/posts/psychedelics) - Brief note framing psychedelics as culturally and consciously consequential.
- [Psychedelic Thinker](/wiki-writings/posts/psychedelicthinker) - Transcript of McKenna reflecting on how psychedelics reshaped his encounter with mind and nature.
- [Dharmapunk](/wiki-writings/posts/dharmapunk) - Internal story concept blending dharmic imagery and speculative/cyber aesthetic into a psychedelic civilizational mode.
