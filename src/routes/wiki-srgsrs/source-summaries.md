This page summarizes every item currently present in `raw/`. Most summaries are based on direct text extraction from title pages, tables of contents, prefaces, or opening chapters; where a PDF is scan-heavy and yielded little machine-readable text, the summary is intentionally conservative and based on the title plus its place in the surrounding corpus.

## By Source

- `An Indictment whose responsibility for the failure of 1942.pdf`: Ram Swarup pamphlet arguing that the failure of the 1942 movement was due to leadership failure rather than popular unwillingness.
- `Catholic Ashrams - Sannyasins or Swindlers - Sita Ram Goel.pdf`: A long critique of Catholic ashrams and Christian indigenisation, portraying them as strategic missionary adaptation rather than spiritual convergence.
- `Defence of Hindu Society - Sita Ram Goel.pdf`: Defense of Hindu society against prophetic exclusivism, with emphasis on spiritual pluralism, civilizational clarity, and self-protection.
- `Foundations of Maoism.pdf`: Ram Swarup's analytical critique of Maoism and the collectivist logic behind communist politics.
- `Freedom-of-Expression.pdf`: Edited volume on censorship, secular theocracy, and controversies over criticizing protected religious or ideological subjects.
- `Gandhi Economics A Supporting Technology.pdf`: Short work linking Gandhian economics with small-scale, supportive, or appropriate technology.
- `Gandhism and communism principles and technique.pdf`: Comparative text contrasting Gandhian and communist methods, principles, and political style.
- `Hindu Society Under Siege by Sita Ram Goel.pdf`: Brief polemical text arguing that Hindu society is under institutional and ideological pressure.
- `Hindu Temples What Happened to Them Vol 1 (1).pdf`: Argumentative first volume on temple destruction, historiographical denial, and the politics of remembering sacred-site desecration.
- `Hindu Temples What Happened to Them Vol 2 - Sita Ram Goel.pdf`: Evidence-focused second volume emphasizing Islamic textual and historical material related to temple destruction.
- `Hindu and Hinduism (Sitaram Goel).pdf`: Short reflection on how the meanings of "Hindu" and "Hinduism" are manipulated in modern discourse.
- `Hindu-Sikh Relationship.pdf`: Short work on the terms of the debate over continuity and difference between Hindu and Sikh traditions.
- `Hindu-view-of-christianity-and-islam-ram-swarup.pdf`: Scan-heavy comparative theology text that appears to present Hindu evaluations of Christianity and Islam.
- `History of Hindu-Christian Encounters (AD 304 to 1996) Sita Ram Goel.docx`: Readable text version of the major survey of Hindu-Christian encounters from late antiquity to 1996.
- `History of Hindu-Christian Encounters (AD 304 to 1996) Sita Ram Goel.pdf`: The same large historical survey in PDF form, expanding the chronology and phases of encounter.
- `History of Hindu-Christian Encounters (AD 304 to 1996).docx`: Zero-byte or broken duplicate of the same title; no readable content.
- `India_s Secularism - Sita Ram Goel.pdf`: Extended critique of Indian secularism, especially the mismatch between European secular language and Indian civilizational realities.
- `Islam Vis -A-Vis Hindu Temple - Sita Ram Goel.pdf`: Scan-heavy temple-focused text that appears, by title and corpus context, to examine Islam's relation to Hindu temples and their destruction.
- `Muslim Separatism - Causes and Consequences - Sita Ram Goel.pdf`: Historical and political explanation of Muslim separatism in India and its deeper ideological roots.
- `On Hinduism - Reviews and Reflections - Ram Swarup.pdf`: Reflective essays defending Hindu thought and dharmic traditions against reductionist and exclusivist frameworks.
- `Perversion of India_s Political Parlance - Sita Ram Goel.pdf`: Study of ideological manipulation in Indian political vocabulary.
- `Pseudo-Secularism (Sitaram Goel).pdf`: Critique of pseudo-secularism centered on Christian missions, the Niyogi Committee, and Hindu resistance.
- `The Calcutta Quran Petition - Sita Ram Goel.pdf`: Large controversy text on Quran criticism, jihad, public reaction, and the legal-political boundaries of critique.
- `The Hindu view of Education.pdf`: Brief statement of a Hindu approach to education and cultural transmission.
- `The Story Of Islamic Imperialism In India by Sita Ram Goel.pdf`: Narrative overview of Islamic conquest and rule in India and the politics of historical memory around it.
- `Theory And Practice Of Muslim State In India - K. S. Lal.pdf`: Detailed study of Muslim political theory, administration, and its Indian historical application.
- `Understanding Islam Through Hadis - Religious Faith or Fanaticism.pdf`: Topical reading of hadith material to show the ritual, legal, and political structure of Islam.
- `Where are they Communists under Communism.pdf`: Short anti-communist tract asking how communists behave when communist rule becomes actual state power.
- `Whither Sikhism (Ram Swarup).pdf`: Brief reflection on Sikh identity, trajectory, and relation to the wider Indic world.
- `Woman+in+Islam+(Ram+Swarup).pdf`: Critical study of women's status in Islam based on religious and social evidence.
- `Word-as-revelation-names-of-god-8185990689.pdf`: Scan-heavy comparative theological text that appears to address revelation, divine names, and the contrast between exclusivist and plural religious vocabularies.
- `jesus-christ-an-artifice-for-aggression-by-sita-ram-goel.pdf`: Polemical critique of Jesus and Christian expansion as civilizational instruments of aggression.
- `papacy-its-doctrine-and-history.pdf`: Short doctrinal and historical critique of the papacy and Roman Catholic authority.
